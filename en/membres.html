This section presents the membership and met à disposition the documents utiles for the members and collaborateurs. the objective : clarté, transparency and participation.

Join and participation

This that signifie be member

  • Get involved à a projet citoyen structuré and transparent
  • Respecter the rules of integrity, of conduite and of accountability
  • Contribuer : idées, organisation, diffusion, soutien, projets
  • Favoriser a climat respectueux and constructif

Principes of base

  • Transparency of documents and of rules
  • Refus of pratiques of influence and of manipulation
  • Priorité à the intérêt public and à the consistency of cadres
  • Respect of citoyens, of the débat and of faits
